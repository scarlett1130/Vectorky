DRILLING
SERVICES

TO TRULY DIFFERENTIATE
OURSELVES FROM OTHER

engineering firms in the market, we own and operate our own fleet of Drill Rigs for soil and rock sampling, and for collection of soil and rock specimens for laboratory testing. By owning and operating our own equipment, we can better control the quality of the specimen(s) retrieved, and quickly adjust our boring program should our field engineer decide additional borings are required without having to obtain permission from the client. This feature alone, makes us one of the Top Drill Rig Companies in Kentucky.

OUR FLEET CONSISTS OF THE FOLLOWING RIGS, WITH A TWO OR THREE-MAN CREW, DEPENDING ON THE SCHEDULE:

  • A CME-45C on a Rubber Tracked Carrier (ATV) and a CME-55 on an International 4-Wheel Drive Truck. The ATV rigs allows us to access hard-to-reach sites and eliminate the need for a bulldozer to move the equipment around. All of the CME rigs are equipped with an Automatic Hammer.
