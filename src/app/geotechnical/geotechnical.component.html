THE GEOTECHNICAL
ENGINEERING DEPARTMENT

at Vector Engineers performs a wide range of services in line with client and project requirements. Vector Engineers is one of the Top Geotechnical Engineering Firms in Kentucky. A CORE SPECIALTY IN THIS AREA INCLUDES OUR ANALYTICAL LABORATORY.

Fully accredited by AMRL since 1999, our soils lab regularly performs tests on undisturbed and remolded soil samples, as well as strength and durability tests on rock samples. The state-of-the-art equipment that allows us to achieve this include - consolidometers, triaxial and direct shear apparatus, permeability devices, soil compaction and classification equipment, and other lab testing equipment.
