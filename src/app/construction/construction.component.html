WHEN IT COMES TO
CONSTRUCTION SERVICES

our staff of experienced personnel and accurate equipment are the reason why we are the partner of choice for so many firms in the states where we operate. We prepare and evaluate all types of construction materials, from a diverse array of bituminous concrete mixes to Portland Cement and even clay, to arrive at optimization studies that ensure the proper mix design for the specific project.

We are also one of the few Construction Services Firms that specialize in petrographic examinations of construction materials; they are microscopic evaluations that allow us to minutely evaluate concrete quality and determine the cause and degree of concrete failures if any, along with potential future performance.

Lastly, besides the standard ASTM tests of absorption, strength, dimension unit weights, and more, we also provide numerous specialty tests on concrete blocks and bricks. These tests include the fire rating test specified by the BOCA & efflorescence test needed by many leading architectural firms.
